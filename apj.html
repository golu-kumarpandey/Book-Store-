Wings of Fire

by Arun Tiwari

"Wings of Fire" is an autobiography written by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, who served as the President of India from 2002 to 2007. The book provides insights into his life, starting from his childhood in a small town to his contributions to India's space and missile programs. "Wings of Fire" is a compelling account of Dr. Kalam's journey from a humble background to becoming a renowned scientist and a beloved President of India.
