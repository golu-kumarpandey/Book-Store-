The 3 mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bhagat

"The 3 Mistakes of My Life" is a novel by Chetan Bhagat, not a non-fiction work, and as of my last knowledge update in January 2022, I can provide you with a summary of the book. Summary of "The 3 Mistakes of My Life": The novel tells the story of three close friends, Govind, Ishaan, and Omi, who live in the city of Ahmedabad, Gujarat. The story is set against the backdrop of significant real-life events in India, including the earthquake in Bhuj and the Gujarat riots.
