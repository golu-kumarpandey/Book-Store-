Harry Potter

by J.K.Rowling

The series begins with "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" (or "Philosopher's Stone" in some regions), where we are introduced to Harry Potter, an orphaned boy who learns on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He is accepted to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he makes friends like Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley and discovers his extraordinary abilities. He also learns about his past: he is the only known survivor of a curse by the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who killed Harry's parents.
